  • Serves: 10

Dijon mustard? cup
ground black pepper ? teaspoon
white sugar ? teaspoon

Step 1: Stir Greek seasoning and water together in a small bowl; let stand until seasoning is moistened, about 5 minutes.
Step 2: Crush the garlic clove with the side of a knife; sprinkle kosher salt over garlic and mash garlic and salt together into a paste with the edge of the knife.
Step 3: Whisk garlic mixture, olive oil, wine vinegar, lemon juice, lemon zest, Dijon mustard, pepper, and sugar into Greek seasoning mixture until combined.

Tzatziki Sauce

  • Time: 15
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
cucumbers 2 medium
Greek yogurt 1 (16 ounce) container plain
garlic, minced 3 cloves
olive oil 2 tablespoons
chopped fresh dill 1 tablespoon
lemon juice? medium
salt and ground black pepperto taste

Step 1: Peel and seed the cucumbers, then grate them with a box grater. Squeeze out the excess liquid.
Step 2: Mix the grated cucumber with the remaining ingredients. Stir until well-combined.
Step 3: For the best flavor, chill the tzatziki in the fridge for at least one hour before serving.
